Know your Akan name!

In Ghana, children are frequently named according to the day of the week they are born. It is part of their culture.
These "day names" have further meaning concerning the soul and the character of the person.
The world has gotten so complex that many of us have lost touch with such aspects of our culture.

In case, sometimes, you wonder what your Akan name is(according to this culture)...then you are in the right place.
